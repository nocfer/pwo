Global UX #1
The workout follows you everywhere
Today a workout only exists on the session screen — leave it and you lose your place. This adds a persistent active-workout surface: a mini-bar docked above the tab bar on every screen, an iOS Live Activity on the lock screen, and a Dynamic Island live countdown. Rest, glance, switch apps — the timer keeps going and you're one tap from back in.
